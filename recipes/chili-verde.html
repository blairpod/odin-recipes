Pork Chili Verde


Tender smoked pork chili verde


Ingredients

  • Approximately 3lbs boneless pork shoulder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 16oz jar of salsa verde
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • optional tortillas and rice/beans for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat pellet grill to 225 F
  2. Season Pork generously
  3. Smoke pork for 1 hour
  4. Increase temperature to 350 F and cook until internal temperature reaches 160 F
  5. Remove pork from grill and rest for 15 minutes
  6. Cut pork into small cubes and remove excess fat/gristle
  7. Blend all other ingredients with a 1/4 cup of water to make sauce
  8. Add sauce and cubed pork to cast iron pan
  9. Place pan back on grill at 350 F for 20 minutes or until meat is tender
  10. Serve with (or without) tortillas and sides
