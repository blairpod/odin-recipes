Smoked Chicken


Easy smoked chicken legs for all!


Ingredients

  • Chicken legs (as many as will fit on the grill 10-12 on ours)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbps olive oil
  • Optional garlic salt or additional seasoning
  • Optional BBQ sauces

Steps

  1. Preheat pellet grill to 275 F
  2. Rinse and pat dry chicken legs
  3. Rub olive oil on chicken
  4. Season chicken to taste
  5. Place chicken directly on grill
  6. Smoke chicken for 30 minutes
  7. Turn legs and smoke an additional 30 minutes
  8. Raise grill temperature to 350 F until internal temp is 155 F
  9. Optionally add sauces now and bring internal temperature up to 165 F
