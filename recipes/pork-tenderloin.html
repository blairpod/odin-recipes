Pork Tenderloin


Easy smoked pork tenderloin in about an hour!


Ingredients

  • One or more port tenderloin unseasoned
  • Cavenders greek seasoning

Steps

  1. Remove the pork tenderloin from the refrigerator
  2. season on all sides with Cavenders
  3. Let meat come to room temperature
  4. Start pellet smoker on smoke with lid open
  5. Preheat at 225 degrees F
  6. Smoke pork at 200-225 until internal temperature reaches 145 degrees F (about 45 minutes)
  7. Remove from heat and rest meat for 10 minutes
  8. Slice and serve!
